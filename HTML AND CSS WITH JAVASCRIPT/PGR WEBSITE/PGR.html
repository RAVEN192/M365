PUNISHING GRAY RAVEN

Punishing: Gray Raven is a 3D post-apocalyptic and sci-fi action RPG where you play as the commandant of the Gray Raven unit. In the game, you assemble an elite team of constructs to lead in intense action combat missions. The game’s narrative revolves around unraveling the dark truths behind a virus disaster known as The Punishing.
