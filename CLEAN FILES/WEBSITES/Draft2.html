Mobile Legends: Bang Bang (MLBB) is a highly popular multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game developed and published by Moonton. Released in 2016, the game is designed for mobile platforms, offering fast-paced, 5v5 battles where two teams compete to destroy each other’s base while defending their own. Players choose from a wide array of heroes, each with unique abilities and roles such as tanks, mages, marksmen, supports, and assassins. Matches typically last around 10-15 minutes, making it a quick and intense gaming experience.
